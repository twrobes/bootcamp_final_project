Welcome to Composer Club

A place for musicians, by musicians

About Composer Club

Musicians love to find and learn music. On this website, we enable people to organize all of their repertoire in one spot. No more going on each website to find all your music! In addition, we hope to cultivate a great community through the forums, where musicians can help others by sharing their experience and knowledge.

  • Create playlists with custom names.
  • Update and delete playlists.
  • Add scores and sheet music to your playlists.
  • Access all your music on one page.

Services we offer

Create playlists, add sheet music to the website, and add music to your playlists all while being able to edit and organize all the music you know and want to learn in one spot.
