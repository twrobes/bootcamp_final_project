About Composer Club

Musicians love to find and learn music. On this website, we enable people to organize all of their repertoire in one spot. No more going on each website to find all your music! In addition, we hope to cultivate a great community through the forums, where musicians can help others by sharing their experience and knowledge.

  • Create playlists with your sheet music and tablature
  • Responsive behavior when clicking nav links perfect for a one page website
  • Bootstrap's scrollspy feature which highlights which section of the page you're on in the navbar
  • Minimal custom CSS so you are free to explore your own unique design options
